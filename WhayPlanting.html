Whay Planting ?

Thousands of plant species are planted for aesthetic purposes:

  • providing shade
  • adjusting heat
  • dampening noise
  • providing privacy
  • preventing soil erosion
  • reducing wind

Plants are at the heart of a multi-billion-dollar-a-year tourism movement
that includes travel to historic parks, national parks, rainforests, and forests with
colorful leaves in the fall, and festivals like the cherry blossom festivals
in America and Japan.
Some gardens are planted with food crops, while many are grown for aesthetic,
ornamental and bio-conservation purposes.
